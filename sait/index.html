BADABOOM - a new level in DATING

Badaboom

Badaboom is a new dating application that uses the most advanced Web 3.0 technologies, including artificial intelligence, neural networks, blockchain technologies and more.

Neural Networks

Neural networks will block prohibited content, detect bots and learn from users, finding patterns and relationships between people to further form a strong pair.

Travel System

With one click you can be anywhere in the world and language will not be a barrier, because translations will occur automatically.

NFT Generator

Create your own NFT images, exchange and sell them to other users. Leave a memory about the people you meet.

BDB Token

The application will use its own crypto token. Shopping, games, weddings and everything else will be purchased for it.

Games for Two

A little excitement will help to avoid dry communication between partners by setting the right communication vector and setting both of them up for a more interesting acquaintance.

Blockchain Wedding

No need to go to Las Vegas, but only 2 smartphones that will arrange a ceremony, with a photo shoot and secured promises, on the blockchain network.

Artificial Intelligence

The application will be equipped with Artificial Intelligence, in the form of an electronic partner, with which lonely people can learn to communicate and simply carry on a conversation.
